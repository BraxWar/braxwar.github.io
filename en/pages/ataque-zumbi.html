Zombie Attack

About the event

In the Zombie Attack event, players and alliances face hordes of zombies to earn points and rewards. The more damage you deal, the greater your contribution to the alliance ranking.

Tips to maximize damage:

  • Use heroes with area-of-effect (AoE) damage skills
  • Activate attack buffs before each wave
  • Join during hours when the alliance concentrates attacks
  • Spend attack tickets strategically, not all at once
