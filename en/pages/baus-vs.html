VS Chests

About the event

VS Chests are one of the main ways to score during the Server vs Server event. Every chest opened or collected contributes to your individual and alliance score.

To maximize your points, focus on:

  • Opening high-value chests during bonus time windows
  • Coordinating with the alliance to share rare chest locations
  • Using speedups and buffs before intensive collection sessions
  • Prioritizing chests that grant VS score over common resources
