Capitol

About the event

The battle for the Capitol is one of the most important events in Last War. The server that controls the Capitol gains significant advantages and prestige among alliances.

Prepare in advance:

  • Strengthen your troops and heroes before the battle begins
  • Join strategy meetings organized by the alliance
  • Be active during peak hours when the fight is most intense
  • Coordinate group attacks to break enemy defenses
