SVS — Server vs Server

About the event

SVS (Server vs Server) is the great war between servers. The entire alliance must work together to defeat the enemy server and accumulate as many points as possible.

Strategies to score in SVS:

  • Participate in all sub-events that generate VS score
  • Attack enemy players when they are unprotected
  • Defend alliance members and strategic buildings
  • Use all buffs and speedups during war days
