Desert Storm

About the event

Desert Storm is a PvP event where alliances compete for control of strategic buildings on the desert map. Scoring depends on occupation, defense and successful attacks.

Essential tips for the alliance:

  • Set entry and exit times coordinated with the alliance leader
  • Prioritize central buildings that generate more points per minute
  • Keep defense troops on conquered positions
  • Scout before attacking heavily defended positions
