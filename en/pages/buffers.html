Buffers

About buffs

Buffers (buffs) are temporary bonuses that increase attack power, defense, march speed and resource production. Knowing when and which buffs to use makes all the difference in war events.

Quick buff guide:

  • Activate attack buffs before PvP battles and war events
  • Use defense buffs when your base is under threat
  • Combine march buffs with surprise attacks in SVS
  • Save rare buffs for the most critical moments of the event
